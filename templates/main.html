Welcome to Readers Hub!

In Readers Hub, users will be able to access and view Reviews and Ratings for over 5000 books.

Feel free to search for books, leave reviews for individual books, and see the reviews made by other users!

Since we are relatively new, we have pulled in ratings from Goodreads, another book review website, so ratings are from a broader audience.

Finally, users will be able to query for book details and book reviews programmatically via the API. Details and documentation in the API tab.
